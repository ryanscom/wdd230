Description of Target Audience

  • Who: Anyone who wants to promote their business and network with other local businesses. People looking for local events.
  • Age: 16 and up.
  • Accessability: Phones, tablets, laptop and desktop computers.

Personas

Person 1: John is the owner of a small, new IT business. He visits the local chamber of commerce website frequently to network with other businesses. John hopes to find those that need IT services so he can fulfill that need and expand his business. John is well versed in using computers, tablets, and phones in his personal and professional life. (He also is afraid of hights, clowns, and cracks in the sidewalk.)

Person 2: Gretchen is a 60-year-old flower shop owner. She is not very comfortable using phones or any electronic device smaller than her business computer. Gretchen wants to find ways of advertising her business that are cost effective. She is hoping her local chamber of commerce will connect her with advertisement opportunities and events. (She is also secretly addicted to fudge brownies).

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenarios that describe the visitors purpose in visiting and using the site:

  1. Advertise your business locally.
  2. Become a member of the Chamber of Commerce.
  3. Check upcoming events.
  4. Find a list of businesses in the area and their contact information.
  5. Get involved in local revitalization projects.
